Our Values

What we stand for

Every decision we make comes back to these principles.

Community First

We build for real people in our community — not algorithms, investors, or trends. Every product decision starts with asking: does this serve our members?

Excellence Without Compromise

We hold ourselves to a high standard in everything — the quality of our professionals, the design of our product, and the care we put into every member interaction.

Authenticity

We say what we mean and mean what we say. We celebrate our culture, our community, and our individuality — and we want our team to bring their full, authentic selves to work.

Accountability

We own our wins and our mistakes equally. A culture of accountability means we grow faster, communicate better, and trust each other more deeply.

Move With Purpose

We're a startup — we move fast. But every step is intentional. We don't ship things just to ship them; we build with care and urgency at the same time.

Growth Mindset

We invest in our people. That means feedback, resources, and real opportunities to grow — not just in title but in skill, confidence, and impact.

Why TDC

What we offer our team

We take care of the people who take care of our community.

Complimentary TDC Membership

All full-time team members receive a free Luxe Glam membership. We believe in our product and want you to experience it fully.

Flexible & Remote Work

Most roles can be done remotely or on a hybrid schedule. We care about output and impact — not where you sit.

Growth & Learning Budget

Every team member receives an annual budget for courses, conferences, books, and tools that support their professional development.

Small Team, Real Impact

You won't get lost here. Every person on the team contributes directly to what TDC becomes — your fingerprints will be on everything we build.

Competitive Compensation

We benchmark salaries to be competitive with market rates. We believe great people deserve to be paid well — no games, no lowballing.

Inclusive Culture

TDC was built to celebrate our community. Our team reflects that — diverse, vibrant, and grounded in shared values of respect and belonging.
